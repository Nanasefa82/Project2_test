Welcome to the Ghanaian Students Association at UNCC Events.

Ghanaian Students Association is an organization that aims to create a community for past, future, and current Ghanaian students of UNC Charlotte. Our goal is to support current and future Ghanaian students at UNC Charlotte by providing a community and a network that fosters peer learning, information sharing, community support, mentorship, career advising, and friendship. As a Ghanaian group at UNC Charlotte, GSA, will also provide a platform for local and international students who want to learn about Ghanaian culture. GSA is open to anyone interested in learning about our beautiful country.
